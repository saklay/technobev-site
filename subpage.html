Our History

As a strong independent bottler and co-packer of soft drinks, water, juices, functional drinks, alcoholic products, and other beverages, TechnoBev has been successfully serving customers in North America since 1924.

Under the name of Liqueurs Bergeron, the company started serving local clients in the greater Montreal area of St-Felix-de-Valois, in Quebec, Canada. Due to continuous growth and the rising popularity of its KIRI brand cola, Liqueur Bergeron changed its name in the 1960's to Breuvages KIRI.

Through many developments and acquisitions, Breuvages KIRI became a successful regional independent bottler with a distribution service to food and convenience stores in the Lanaudière and Montreal regions, and directly to homes in Quebec, northwestern New Brunswick, and southeastern Ontario.

As KIRI grew, the company became increasingly involved in co-packing and private branding activities as clients sought out KIRI's expertise and reputation for quality. The offering of product development and bottling services for other companies would eventually take on such importance, that Breuvages KIRI would be forced to restructure itself and invest further in order to meet growing customer needs.

All the while maintaining the KIRI brand name, Breuvages KIRI became TechnoBev, a highly versatile, experienced, and professional beverage co-packer with the latest production and laboratory technology.
